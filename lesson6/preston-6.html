We can save the Earth. Here's how

We hear a lot about how terrible the world is today. We risk flipping ourselves out of the goldilocks “Holocene” period of predictable warm weather (which has allowed humans to flourish during the last 12,000 years) and into a highly risky “hothouse earth” scenario, with uncontrolled feedback driving faster warming and more droughts and storms.
