Design Principles

PARC: Alignment

Refinitiv

In Refinitiv the website in general is minimalist and use a well UX Design where the user can easily navigate through the page. It uses the Principle of Alignment both in desktop and mobile. Seeing the mobile version it uses edge alignment and centered.

PARC: Repetition

I choose American Motors for the Principle of Repetition because when you navigate through all the pages it's harmonious , the colors which are mainly blue , white and red . In addition to that , the container and the repetition on the size of the containers that uses for the cars and info

White Space and Clean Design

Airbnb

One main characteristic of Airbnb it's the white space and Clean Design , there's no so much space that makes it empty . Contrary to that it gives a fresh and simple interaction to the user
