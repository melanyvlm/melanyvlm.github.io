Target Audience

The StayWeather website has as a target audience they both will use desktop and mobile :

  • Who : Students , People interested in the weather in different cities
  • Age : People from 15 - 65+
  • Income: Income levels apply from 16+(it depends from the country where it's used because of the majority of age) and it's between $10.000 and above anually
  • Motivation: Provide information about the current weather and some interesting quotes about how to keep our planet save.

Personas

  • Occupation : Elementary student
  • Goal and motivation for using the site : Information for her Geographic Homework
  • Social : Like to play with her friends , curious for discovering new things
  • Technology : Mostly use her Computer and Ipad to search info and play videogames. Using mainly Chrome as a browser
  • Occupation : Collegue student
  • Goal and motivation for using the site : Information to help his cousins on their homework and curious for weather in other countries
  • Social : Like to hang out with his friends, play video games and spend time with his family and girlfriend.
  • Technology : Mostly use her Computer and Ipad and PC to do his homework and for work. Using mainly Excel , Powerpoint
